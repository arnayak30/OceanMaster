Instructions

Welcome to Ocean Master Trivia!


1. To begin the game, press play.


2. You will then be asked a series of questions.


3. Each question has four answer choices and only one correct answer.


4. Once you select an answer, you cannot change it.


5. Press the "Next Question" button after you answer each question.


6. After you answer all of the questions, you will be given a score that shows you how well you performed in the game.


7. Finally, if you want to play again, just select play and you'll be able to answer more questions. (Note: Some may be repeats)

Our Mission

Our oceans are vital to life on earth. The oceans produce 70% of the world’s oxygen and absorbs 50 times more carbon than our atmosphere. Additionally, the oceans help regulate the earth’s climate, provide food for billions of people, provide millions of jobs, and are home to 50-80% of all life. Despite its importance, the ocean faces more threats than ever, such as run-off pollution, air pollution, oil spills, oxygen depletion, climate change, and overfishing. Most people are completely unaware of the numerous threats to our ocean. Our goal with OceanMaster is to spread awareness about the various environmental issues that are taking place in our society. We hope that this platform can be used to tell people to take initiative on protecting our beautiful oceans.
